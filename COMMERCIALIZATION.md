## The Two Brands

Fireside Labs operates two distinct products under one parent entity.

### 1. Fireside (Consumer) — `getfireside.ai`

**What it is:** A free, privacy-first AI companion that runs entirely on your hardware.

- **Desktop app** (.exe / .app / .AppImage) with RPG-style brain picker, companion animals, overnight learning, and guild hall visualization
- **Mobile app** (iOS / Android) with guardian keyboard, voice, and companion care
- **39 plugins**, 59+ models, mesh networking
- **Revenue:** Marketplace (30% cut on plugins, themes, voice packs, trained agents)
- **Pricing:** Free. Always. No subscription, no hosted tier, no cloud dependency. The local-only model is the differentiator, not a limitation.

### 2. The Foundry (Enterprise) — `firesidelabs.ai`

**What it is:** A proprietary post-training optimization engine for enterprise LLMs.

- **Zero-data model surgery:** Compression, recalibration, and domain grafting without retraining
- **Target:** CTOs, AI leads, regulated industries (finance, healthcare, defense)
- **Revenue:** Per-model optimization engagements ($10K+)
- **Pricing tiers:**

| Service | Description | Price Range |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Brain Scan (Diagnostic) | Synergy map + dead layer report | $2,500 |
| Intelligent Compression | Dead layer pruning + MoE expert merging | $10-15K |
| Full Foundry | Compression + dampening + Oracle Gate routing | $25-40K |
| Organ Grafting | QLoRA domain specialist training + graft + validation | $40-75K |

## What IS Unique (Updated)

### Consumer (Fireside)

1. **Experience-based learning** — procedural memory, dream consolidation, somatic gating. The AI improves from deployment, not from training data.
2. **Two-character system** — AI person at home + companion animal on your phone. Nobody else has this.
3. **Adaptive immunity** — adversarial patterns broadcast to all mesh nodes in real-time. Each attack makes every node stronger.
4. **Guild hall visualization** — infrastructure as a game, not a dashboard.

### Enterprise (The Foundry)

1. **MoE Expert Surgery** — lossless expert merging (151 pairs merged, 0 degradation), domain-specific pruning, expert replacement
2. **Body/Mind/Soul Architecture** — proprietary three-zone model for transformer layer topology
3. **Synergy Chain Mapping** — pairwise interaction analysis that reveals hidden circuit dependencies (competitors doing single-layer ablation will never find these)
4. **Blood Type Compatibility** — L2 divergence scanning for pre-flight graft safety checks
5. **Model Forensics** — self-service IP theft detection via structural divergence analysis

### The Bridge

The Foundry exists because Fireside needed it. To run 30B MoE models locally on consumer hardware, we had to invent radical post-training compression. The consumer app is proof that the enterprise technology works.

## What Is NOT Unique

These components use well-known engineering patterns with good naming:

| System | What It Actually Is |
|---|---|
| Event bus | In-process pub/sub |
| Inference cache | LRU keyed by prompt hash |
| Working memory | Top-K retrieval injected into prompt |
| Circuit breaker | Per-connection failsafe |
| Rate limiting | Token bucket per IP |
| Gossip sync | Timestamp-based LWW merge |
| Cost tracking | Per-call token/USD logging |

Solid implementations. Not the moat. A funded team could reproduce them in weeks.

## The Moat

The moat is **compound and multi-layered:**

1. **Consumer:** Accumulated operational intelligence — 90 days of learned procedures, calibrated somatic markers, and evolved personality traits specific to a user's life. A competitor can copy the code. They cannot copy the relationship.

2. **Enterprise:** The Scanner DB — every model that passes through The Foundry populates an 18-table proprietary atlas of neural architecture physics (dead layers, resonance peaks, synergy chains, expert similarity matrices, blood type compatibility scores). Each scan makes the next optimization faster and more precise. Competitors would need thousands of GPU-hours to reproduce even a fraction of this database.

## IP Protection

- **PUBLISH:** Results only ("We achieved +14% with zero retraining"). Problem statement (Balloon Effect). Product pitch.
- **NEVER PUBLISH:** Any methodology. Synergy mapping. Dampening curves. L2 divergence targeting. The stacking discovery. The Scanner DB. Expert merging thresholds. Dead-end experiments. Forensic analysis methods.
- **Rule:** Show the before-and-after photos. Never show the operating room. Never publish anything that could even remotely hint at how we arrived at our discoveries.
- **Strategy:** Trade secret. No patents (until funded and able to enforce). No expert testimony. No academic papers.
