## Description

### The Pitch

Most AI tools reset every conversation. You explain the same things over and over. They never get better.

**Valhalla is different.** It runs on your hardware, learns from every interaction, and stress-tests its own knowledge overnight. Day 90 is measurably smarter than day 1 — without retraining the model.

### How It Works

1. **Install** — One command. Mac, Linux, or Windows. [3 minutes]
2. **Chat** — Talk naturally. It remembers everything.
3. **Sleep** — Overnight, it consolidates what it learned, tests itself, and prunes mistakes.
4. **Wake up smarter** — Check the Learning page: "247 things known · 94% reliable · +12% this week"

### What Makes It Unique

- 🧠 **Overnight learning loop** — Dream consolidation → Knowledge testing → Pruning. Gets better while you sleep.
- 👤 **Soul system** — Each AI has a personality file (SOUL.md). Change one markdown file, change the entire behavior.
- 🏰 **Guild Hall** — Watch your AI agents work in a visual, themed scene. Pick from Norse hall, modern office, space station, cozy room, or pixel dungeon.
- 🎤 **Voice chat** — Optional. Whisper for listening, Kokoro for speaking. Your voice never leaves your computer.
- 📱 **Telegram integration** — Chat with your AI from your phone. Get notifications when tasks complete.
- 🔒 **100% private** — Runs on YOUR hardware. No cloud. No data collection. No subscription.
