## Mode-Specific Tones

### One brand, two energies. NOT two apps.

### Companion Mode 🐾
- **Warm, playful, alive**
- Companion avatar is PROMINENT — large, animated, center of attention
- Bouncy animations, haptic-rich interactions
- Rounded corners, soft shadows, organic shapes
- The fire glows warm and inviting — like a campfire
- Labels: "Companion Mode"

### Executive Mode 💼
- **Warm but refined, sophisticated**
- Same amber palette but DIALED BACK — less saturation, more contrast
- Companion avatar is present but SMALLER — subtle, not bouncing
- Clean typography, sharper corners, more whitespace
- Minimal animation — smooth fades instead of bounces
- The fire becomes a refined ember glow — like a fireplace in a premium office
- Labels: "Executive Mode"

### What stays the same across both modes:
- Same app icon
- Same splash screen
- Same dark background (`--deep-charcoal`)
- Same accent color (`--fire-orange`)
- Same settings screen
- The companion is ALWAYS present — just at different scales

---

## Typography

- **Primary:** Inter or SF Pro (system default on iOS)
- **Companion mode:** Regular weight, friendly sizing
- **Executive mode:** Medium/Semibold weight, slightly tighter tracking

---

## The Test

If an exec opens the app and sees a bouncing cartoon: ❌ FAIL.
If a kid opens the app and it feels like a boring productivity tool: ❌ FAIL.
The mode switch should feel like going from a cozy living room to a warm office. Same house, different room.
